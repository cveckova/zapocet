Title:On distance distribution functions-valued submeasures related to aggregation functions

Authors:Lenka Halčinová, Ondrej Hutník, Radko Mesiar
(Submitted on 17 Jul 2011)
Abstract: Probabilistic submeasures generalizing the classical (numerical) submeasures are introduced and discussed in connection with some classes of aggregation functions. A special attention is paid to triangular norm-based probabilistic submeasures and semi-copula-based probabilistic submeasures. Some algebraic properties of classes of such submeasures are also studied.
Comments: 16 pages, submitted for publication
Subjects: Classical Analysis and ODEs (math.CA); Probability (math.PR)
MSC classes: 60A10
